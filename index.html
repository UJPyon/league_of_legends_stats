What is League of Legends?

×

League of Legends is a fast-paced popular online multiplayer game that involves working as a team of 5 against another team to take down the opposing team's base. As a highly competitive game, many people often look toward statistical comparisons on how they perform early on in a game compared to those at a higher skill level.

This project aims to provide a sample selection of data collected from games across different skill levels as a visual comparison of how players in different leagues stack against each other.

What's being displayed?

×

The game map shows patterns of player deaths in the first 15 minutes in a sample selection of matches ranging from the lowest tier of ranked play (iron tier) to the highest level tier (challenger tier) from left to right. The breakdown of average stats per game's first 15 minutes, such as total gold earned, wards placed, and towers destroyed on the map are graphed to show the differences in numbers of how different level divisions perform within the early stages of the game.
